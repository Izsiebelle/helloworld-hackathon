About

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is a problem solving, innovation, coding expereince. Hacking just means creative problem solving (ex, life hacks). Some people come to a hackathon to learn how to code, others to brush up on their skills and others to compete. Regardless of your motive, all are welcome to join us.

Where can I learn to code?

Coding is such a fantastic skill and there are many different ways to learn. In person class in schools are fantastic, but we know not everyone has access to them yet. At the "Hello, World!" Hackathon you will be able to learn from some amazing mentors that work as software engineers and developers right here in Kansas City. In the meantime, however, there are a ton of great online resources like Codeacademy and Khanacademy We’ve put together a beginner’s tutorial which will give you all the background information you need to get started.
