About

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is a problem solving, innovation, coding expereince. Hacking just means creative problem solving (ex, life hacks). Some people come to a hackathon to learn how to code, others to brush up on their skills and others to compete. Regardless of your motive, all are welcome to join us.

Where can I learn to code?

Coding is such a fantastic skill and there are many different ways to learn. In person class in schools are fantastic, but we know not everyone has access to them yet. At the "Hello, World!" Hackathon you will be able to learn from some amazing mentors that work as software engineers and developers right here in Kansas City. In the meantime, however, there are a ton of great online resources like Codeacademy and Khanacademy We’ve put together a beginner’s tutorial which will give you all the background information you need to get started. Just click here!

FAQ

  • What is a hackathon?
  • A hackathon is a problem solving, innovation, coding expereince. Hacking just means creative problem solving (ex, life hacks). Some people come to a hackathon to learn how to code, others to brush up on their skills and others to compete. Regardless of your motive, all are welcome to join us.
  • What is the goal of this event?
  • To introduce young women to the world of computing and to build to sisterhood.
  • Do I need to know how to code before attending this event?
  • Absolutely not! Come as you are and we will teach you the rest!
  • How do I form a group/team?
  • You can make a group of up to four before you come to the invent or we will have time for you to meet others and form groups at the beginning of the event!
  • What do I need to bring?
  • If you have a computer please bring it! Other wise bring yourself, comfy clothes and if you want a sleepingbag, blanket, or pillow.
  • Do I have to attend the whole time?
  • We understand that their are family emergencies and that things come up, but we do ask that you commit to the full 24 hours.
  • When do I need to register by?
  • The last day to register is February 29th.
